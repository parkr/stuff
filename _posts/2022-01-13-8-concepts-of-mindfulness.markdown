[Radio Headspace](https://overcast.fm/itunes1510981488/radio-headspace) is
a podcast from [Headspace](https://headspace.com), a very popular
meditation app.

[Kessonga Giscombe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNM0W3GQ5zw), a
meditation teacher on Headspace, hosts 8 episodes to start off the new year
about the 8 concepts of mindfulness:

- [**Non-judging**: *New Year, New Me*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520100346/https://overcast.fm/+aeiTSbGbA/1:29)
- [**Patience**: _Patience Takes Practice_](https://overcast.fm/+aeiTJ-wgY)
- [**Acceptance**: *Accept Where You Are*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520094838/https://overcast.fm/+aeiSYupug)
- [**Trust**: *Trust Yourself*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520100735/https://overcast.fm/+aeiT7nECU)
- [**Non-striving**: *Non-Striving Attitude*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520112639/https://overcast.fm/+aeiQdf03E)
- [**Letting Go**: _Lost In Thought_](https://overcast.fm/+aeiSXIMxA)
- [**Beginner's Mind**: *New Beginnings*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520101233/https://overcast.fm/+aeiQSs1uU)
- [**Humor**: *The Healing Power of Laughter*](https://web.archive.org/web/20220520111837/https://overcast.fm/+aeiTK7Dxw)

They're well-done, and they're pithy (~5 minutes each). Go listen!
